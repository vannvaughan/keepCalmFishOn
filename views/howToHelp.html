How You Can Help

Volunteering

Keep Calm Fish On is always looking for volunteers to help out and dedicate a portion of their time to conservation or recovery efforts. If you are interested in volunteering, feel free to submit your information below and we will let you know when we have another event come up.

Donations

If you can't make it to an event, we could always use donations. Keep Calm Fish On is a non-profit organization where every penny counts and goes towards the goal of a safe and clean ocean as well as the assistance of those who have been in the unfortunate path of mother nature.
